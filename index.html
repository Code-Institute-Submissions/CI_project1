WE ARE RYZN

UFC Gym Singapore is a brand extension of the UFC and UFC Athletes which are believed to be the best-conditioned athletes in the world. UFC Gym is a place where you can train with scientifically developed UFC training modules which are toned down to suit your individual fitness levels at UFC Gym. We believe in empowering the fighting spirit in you to be able to face your individual fights such as fat loss, better health and looking better. UFC Gym provides a fun, clean and safe environment where you can train different with the safety of a coach alongside your friends, family and community. We are different from any other gyms because we have 3 aspects of fitness which comprises of Martial Arts (Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Boxing, Muay Thai) which you will learn multiple disciplines, self-defence skills, and mental strength, Functional Fitness such as our DUT classes which you will learn mobility and endurance, and a weights area where you can strengthen and condition your muscles to help prevent injuries.
